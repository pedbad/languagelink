Welcome to the LanguageLink Advising System

Built by the eLearning team at the University of Cambridge Language Centre, LanguageLink helps students and advisers connect: schedule sessions, prepare together, and track progress in one place.

For students

Find the right adviser, share your goals, and arrive prepared. Keep everything in one place—from your initial questionnaire to follow-up notes and resources after each session.

  • Book from real-time adviser availability
  • Questionnaire surfaces goals and needs early
  • Revisit shared notes and links anytime

For teachers

Publish availability, see student context at a glance, and keep tidy records with session notes and shared resources. Simple tools that support high-quality advising.

  • Manage availability and bookings effortlessly
  • Student profiles and questionnaires in one view
  • Rich-text notes and resource sharing after sessions
